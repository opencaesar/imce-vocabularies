Abstract

The Project vocabulary defines concepts and properties for describing a project in terms of its stakeholders, concerns, work breakdown, and deliverables. It provides a basis for specialization by domain experts.

4. Concepts

4.1. Authority

An Authority is any entity that asserts a fact in a model. Example Authoritys include Program, Project, and WorkPackage. (The association of an Authority with its asserted facts requires mechanisms outside the vocabulary of OML and OWL proper, e.g., named graphs. Authorization is conveyed by the organization of statements in a repository.)
